About

Designed by Nicolau Nasoni, the Torre dos Clérigos is the bell tower of the Clérigos Church and one of the tallest in Portugal. Its granite spiral staircase of 240 steps offers panoramic views across the Douro and the historic centre — a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1996.

About

Famous for its blue-and-white azulejo tile facade added in 1912, Igreja do Carmo sits beside the slimmest house in Porto — a narrow building just over a metre wide, built to separate it from the neighbouring Carmelitas church.
